Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Got questions about our courses or services? Find answers here.

What photography courses do you offer?

We offer beginner to advanced photography courses, covering everything from camera settings to composition and lighting techniques.

Do you provide hands-on training?

Yes, we offer practical lessons where students can apply what they've learned in real-world scenarios with expert guidance.

What is the duration of the videography course?

The videography course typically lasts for 8 weeks, with both theory and practical sessions included in the curriculum.

Can I take the courses online?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online training options to accommodate students from various locations.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes, all our courses offer certificates upon successful completion, which can be added to your portfolio or resume.

How can I enroll in a course?

You can easily enroll through our website by selecting your desired course and completing the registration process.
